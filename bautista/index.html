UP Astronomical Society
Astronomical Student Organization

The University of the Philippines Astronomical Society (UP Astrosoc) is a non-profit, non-political and non-partisan organization in the University of the Philippines, Diliman devoted to the study and promotion of astronomy to the students.

The organization has membership of students coming from different colleges in the university.
"Ad Astra Per Aspera" - To the stars through difficulties

Mission

UP Astrosoc is dedicated to the promotion and understanding of Astronomy among students and the general public.

It also partakes in several astronomy activities throughout the academic year. These include astronomy classes, observing sessions, public astronomy lectures,exhibits and forums.
